About Inspire CAD Academy & School of Design

At Inspire CAD Academy, we aim to bridge the gap between talent and opportunities by offering a wide range of courses that cater to the growing demands of the design and engineering sectors.

To empower the next generation of designers and engineers by providing comprehensive training in various fields like Graphic Designing, Fashion Designing, Architectural Engineering, and more. Our vision is to cultivate creativity, technical skills, and innovation in every student.

About Inspire CAD Academy

Our Mission

At Inspire CAD Academy, our mission is to provide world-class training in CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and Design fields, enabling students to harness their creative potential and technical skills. We believe in fostering a learning environment that promotes creativity, critical thinking, and hands-on experience. Our core values include integrity, innovation, excellence, and a commitment to lifelong learning, ensuring our students not only succeed but thrive in their professional careers.
